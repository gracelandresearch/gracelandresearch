ASSESSMENT

We evaluate our clients' situations carefully and impartially, after which we can recommend one of three posturings.

EXIT

Incumbents face existential risk and the surival of the entire industry is in question. Existing barriers to entry are waning or insufficient. We work with clients to strategically ensure they are not the last ones out.

INTEGRATE

Incumbents and disruptors both enjoy advantages and demonstrate core competencies. We work with clients to identify promising frontrunners and align them with our client so that they may write the future of their industry together.

INCUBATE

Incumbents enjoy secular advantages and have the breathing room to write the future of disruption themselves. We work with clients to forge legacies of experience into visions for the future through rapid prototyping.
